Owl Alert TimePiece:

The OwlAlert TimePiece is a program that aims to keep track of student activities. It’s made so that it can account for student attendance, departures, and health needs using custom-made smartwatches. Upon registering into a school, every student will be receiving an OwlAlert TimePiece smartwatch, and all of their data will be stored in it. It will contain many features such as notifying both guardians and teachers, assisting vendors in catering to every student’s allergies and health status as they make purchases from the canteen, and so much more. Overall, our program shall remove the stepping stones and pave a better future in the education-technology industry.

Cinema Application:

This cinema booking application streamlines the ticket and food purchasing process, reducing long queues and crowds. It allows customers of all ages to easily book tickets and make selections in minutes, offering a fast, efficient, and user-friendly experience. By automating calculations, it minimizes errors and enhances worker efficiency, saving time and improving the overall customer experience.
